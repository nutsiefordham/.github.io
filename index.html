Cricket Man of the Match Calculator

Tired of arguing over who really deserved Man of the Match after your Sunday league thriller? Whether you scored a gritty 12 off 117 balls or took 5 wickets with some absolute filth, this tool is for you. Built for the very average cricketer (you know who you are), this calculator crunches the numbers so you don’t have to. Go on—give yourself the credit you think you deserve... even if you did turn up late.

Scoring Breakdown

  • Run = 1 point
  • Wicket = 20 points
  • Catch = 5 points
  • Economy* (Bowling) × 4
  • Stumping = 5 points
  • Boundary = 1.5 points

*Economy Scoring: We subtract your economy rate from 12 (because if you're going for more than 12 an over, you're losing points sorry), then apply the multiplier.
