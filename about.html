About Me

Hi! I'm nutsiefordham, the creator of the Cricket Player of the Match Calculator. I'm a lifelong cricket tragic, club cricketer, and proud numbers nerd. This project was born out of endless post-match debates and a love for automating the little things that make grassroots sport fun.

My goals with this tool are:

  • Make amateur cricket more fun and fair
  • Help club scorers and captains settle those classic "who won it?" arguments
  • Make a little bit of stats geekery available to everyone

When I'm not scoring cricket or writing code, you might spot me playing guitar, coaching juniors, or enjoying a slow single at deep square leg.
